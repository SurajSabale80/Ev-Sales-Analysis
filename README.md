# ⚡ Electric Vehicle Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Electric Vehicle (EV) Sales Dashboard** developed using Power BI to analyze sales trends, vehicle distribution, and market insights.

The dashboard helps in understanding EV adoption patterns, identifying top-performing brands, and evaluating vehicle categories based on real-world data.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total EV sales and growth trends
* Compare BEV (Battery Electric Vehicles) vs PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles)
* Identify top manufacturers and models
* Understand geographical distribution of EV sales
* Evaluate electric range performance

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & data visualization
* **Excel / CSV** – Data source
* **DAX** – Calculated measures & KPIs
* **Data Cleaning** – Handling inconsistencies and formatting

---

## 📌 Key Dashboard Metrics

* 🚗 **Total Vehicles:** 150K+
* 🔋 **Average Electric Range:** 67.83 Km
* ⚡ **BEV Vehicles:** 117K (78%)
* 🔌 **PHEV Vehicles:** 34K (22%)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 📈 **Year-wise Sales Trend Analysis**
* 🌍 **State-wise Vehicle Distribution (Map Visualization)**
* 🏭 **Top Manufacturers Analysis (Tesla, Nissan, Chevrolet, etc.)**
* 🚘 **Model-wise Breakdown (Model Y, Model 3, Leaf, etc.)**
* 📊 **Vehicle Type Distribution (BEV vs PHEV)**
* 🎛️ **Interactive Filters (City, Electric Utility, Vehicle Type)**

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Tesla dominates the EV market**, contributing the highest number of vehicles
* EV adoption has shown **significant growth over the years**, especially after 2018
* **BEVs account for the majority (78%)**, indicating a strong shift toward fully electric vehicles
* Certain models like **Model Y and Model 3** lead in total sales
* EV distribution is concentrated in specific regions, showing **uneven adoption patterns**
